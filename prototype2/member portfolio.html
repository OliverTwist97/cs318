GCDC helps encourage those interested in Graphic Design to create art that best expresses themselves and their interests. With members ranging from all types of majors/minors and different standings in school, we emphasize the ability to let your creativity flourish. Along with teaching members Adobe programs to create Graphic Art, we also critique our works to help ourselves become better artists with the help of our peers. No one at GCDC is here to put others down or compete with each other. GCDC is a collective community that strives to see all members enjoy and be proud of what they create.
