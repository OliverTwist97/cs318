GCDC and AIGA

One of GCDC's goals is to cement a healthy design community within UWEC. We want to be able to extend opportunities to our members and artists on campus in general such as group portfolio reviews and acknowledging potential job or internship opportunities to better prepare us for our futures. To assist us with our goal, GCDC has formed a student group with AIGA. The American Institute of Graphic Arts, or AIGA, is a nationally recognized organization for designers across the country. Along with job postings and internship opportunities, AIGA is a hub for all types of designers both professional and up-and-coming.
